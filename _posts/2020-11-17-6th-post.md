### 2.3. 호스트 주소 수 구하기

Q. What is the maximum number of IP addresses that can be assigned to hosts on a local subnet using the 255.255.255.128 subnet mask?

- 128
- 60
- **126**
- 62
- 252
- 258
- 124
- 58
- 64

Solution : 2^(32 - 25) - 2 = 128 - 2 = 126
